Our Mission

VisionClerk has created a system that will automatically monitor compliance and help the procurement/finance teams understand potential opportunities through use of Artificial Intelligence. Our platform offers unprecedented efficiency by automating the processing, analysis, and reporting of contract and spend data. We do this by offering a cloud SaaS platform whereby procurement and finance departments can upload contracts, rate cards and invoices, or integrate with existing systems, to intelligently structure data and uncover actionable opportunities. Rather than helping teams visualise data, our focus is on providing actionable opportunities for teams to explore.
