What We Offer

VisionClerk is the fastest and most accurate way for businesses to monitor contract compliance and identify cost savings opportunities. Our platform helps find and deliver value by identifying pricing disparities, enforcing contract compliance and uncovering opportunities for supplier consolidation.

Why Use VisionClerk?

Contract compliance and money saving can often go overlooked. We have a solution for that!

Process

We use AI to extract key pieces of information from your data. This includes contracts, invoices and spend data. We're able to extract start dates, end dates, supplier information and price data to form an accurate and robust data set.

Analyze

Using your data sets we're able to analyse spend patterns to identify key opportunities for you. This includes opportunities for supplier consolidation, benchmark comparisons with others in similar industries and fraudulent transactions.

Report

In every area of our platform we actively seek and deliver actionable insights for your team to explore further and implement. These happen on a live basis and are enabled by industry insight programmed into our platform by our experienced team.

Our Vision

In July 2017, an article was published in the Harvard Business Review stating that 'AI May Soon Replace Even the Most Elite Consultants'. At VisionClerk we believe that time is now. Our goal is not to become a Management Consulting firm, but to build a scaleable platform that negates the need for many consulting engagements. In our first product focused on spend analytics specific to procurement and finance departments we're starting small. We're linking in contract data, invoice data and in many cases PO/AP data. After creating a robust platform we'll be able to uncover additional value for our customers through providing actionable insights by linking in usage data, benchmark comparisons and information on innovative products coming to market.
